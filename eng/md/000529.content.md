## Titus (id: 132098)

A man who worked and travelled with Paul. He was a Greek Gentile believer who wasnâ€™t circumcised. He served in many of the churches that Paul helped start. He was an important leader in the church on the island of Crete. He also helped take the offering given by the Corinthian believers to Jerusalem. The New Testament book called Titus is a letter that Paul wrote to him.
