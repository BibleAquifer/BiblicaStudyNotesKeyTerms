## Asaph (id: 132452)

A Levite from the family line of Gershon. David appointed Asaph, his helpers and the sons from Asaphâ€™s family line. David appointed them to be in charge of worship services. They led the people of Israel in praising God with songs of thanks. They prophesied, played instruments and wrote songs. Some of these songs are recorded in the book of Psalms.
